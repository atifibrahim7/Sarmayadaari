Sarmaya karo

A Low Risk way to Invest your Money

At Sarmaya karo, we believe in empowering individuals to take control of their financial future. Whether you're a student just starting out, a freelancer managing your own business, or a woman looking to make smart financial decisions, our platform is designed to help you invest with confidence.

What we intend to do

To democratize investment opportunities and make financial growth accessible to everyone particular Students and Women accessable from thier mobile phone without the need of a bank account. We aim to create a supportive community where students, freelancers, and women can confidently invest their savings and achieve their financial goals from their mobile phone.

  • Multiple Investment Options

    Each time a digital asset is purchased or sold, Sequoir donates a percentage of the fees back

  • Expert Guidance

    We aim to accumulate a team of financial experts on our platform to provide you with the insights required to make informed investment decisions.

  • Seamless Integration with Digital Wallets

    We aim to facilitate investments through popular digital wallets available in Pakistan, making the process convenient and secure. Manage your investments effortlessly, right from your digital wallet.

Problem Statement:

"Students and women face barriers to investing, including the requirement of a bank account and lack of financial literacy. These barriers prevent them from growing their savings and achieving financial independence."

Our Solution:

We aim to develop a mobile platform that allows users to invest easily and securely using digital wallets. Our platform will provide educational resources and a supportive community to help users make informed investment decisions.

  • Our Intended Product

    Shariah-Compliant Mutual Funds,
    Easy and Fast Account Setup,
